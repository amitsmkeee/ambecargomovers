Ambe Cargo Movers

Ambe Cargo Movers is establised in the year 2008, it is under partnership of Abhishek Kumar and Gopal Lal Das. Ambe Cargo is one of the high performing Packers and Movers Company in the area of moving industry across the Nation and it is highlighted as a Best Packers and Movers in Chennai, Tamilnadu, India. Our vision is to offer world-class service of relocation and packers and Movers in Chennai NCR and all over India.


We provide services like Transportation services from Chennai to all India for all kind of vehicles, Lcv open truck services, Taraus services, Trailer transport services, Trailer services for import and export purpose, 20ft & 32ft container single axel and multi axel services, Railway transportation services, Project equipment handling etc,.
